<style type="text/css">/*<![CDATA[*/ .ProdHead { color:#565656; } .focus { color:#565656; font-weight:bold; } /*]]>*/</style><div id="section_1" class="mt-page-section"><span id="Overview"></span><h2 class="editable">Overview</h2> <p>This release notes provides a summary of&nbsp;<strong>Zuora Quotes Release</strong> (Q4&nbsp;2014), including new features, enhancements, and resolved issues.</p> <p>The&nbsp;release is part of the Zuora for Salesforce Q4 2014 release that includes:</p> <ul> <li>Zuora Quotes, version 6.30, 6.30.1, 6.30.2</li> <li>Zuora 360, version 2.100<br /> See&nbsp;<a title="Zuora 360 Q3 2014 Release Notes" rel="internal" href="https://zuora-prod2.mindtouch.us/B_New_Features/AB_360Sync_Release_Notes">&nbsp;Zuora&nbsp;360 Release Notes</a>&nbsp;for the list of new features and enhancements in the new release of Zuora 360.</li> </ul> <p>To use the 6.30 release of&nbsp;Zuora Quotes, you must upgrade your package as described in&nbsp;<a title="Upgrade Zuora Quotes to Q4 2014" rel="internal" href="https://zuora-prod2.mindtouch.us/CB_Commerce/Zuora_for_Salesforce/C_Z-Force_Quotes/00A_ZuoraSales_Quotes_Upgrade_Guide">Upgrade Zuora Quotes to Q4 2014</a>.</p> <div id="section_2" class="mt-page-section"><span id="Zuora_Quotes_Highlights"></span><h3 class="editable">Zuora Quotes Highlights</h3> <p>Some of the highlights in the Q4&nbsp;2104 release of Zuora Quotes are:</p> <ul class="RN"> <li><span class="focus">Product and Price Rules:&nbsp;</span>The Product and Price&nbsp;Rules provide you a guided and controlled product selection and accurate quoting&nbsp;experience. You can manage and offer your users dynamic pricing, automatic addition and removal of rate plans, and error checking in the Product Selector.</li> <li><span class="focus">Hosted Payment Pages Enhancements:</span>&nbsp;Payment Pages 2.0, a&nbsp;new and improved version of hosted payment pages,&nbsp;can be integrated into your quoting process. You can create Payment Pages to match the look and feel of your checkout flow. And your customized Payment Pages will allow your subscribers to send payment details directly to Zuora in a secure, PCI-compliant manner.</li> </ul> </div></div><div id="section_3" class="mt-page-section"><span id="New_Features_and_Enhancements"></span><h2 class="editable">New Features and Enhancements</h2> <div id="section_4" class="mt-page-section"><span id="Product_and_Price_Rules_for_Product_Selector"></span><h3 dir="ltr" class="editable">Product and Price Rules for Product Selector</h3> <p>Product and price rules are for companies with complex, extensive product catalogs. They allow granular control of the product selection&nbsp;process through standard Salesforce&nbsp;configuration. &nbsp;</p> <p>With product rules, you have a better control over product and rate plan selections. Product rules check&nbsp;that the products and rate plans your users select will result in a correct, streamlined quote for the downstream processes. &nbsp;</p> <p>Price rules offer your users dynamic pricing during the quoting process by allowing standard pricing and custom fields to be altered through Salesforce&nbsp;configuration.</p> <p dir="ltr"><span>See&nbsp;<a title="Product and Price Rules" rel="internal" href="https://zuora-prod2.mindtouch.us/CB_Commerce/Zuora_for_Salesforce/C_Z-Force_Quotes/Product_and_Price_Rules">Product and Price Rules</a>&nbsp;for detail about Product and Price Rules.</span></p> </div><div id="section_5" class="mt-page-section"><span id="Payment_Pages_Enhancements"></span><h3 dir="ltr" class="editable">Payment Pages Enhancements</h3> <p>You can create&nbsp;and use, in Zuora Quotes, the new version of hosted payment pages&nbsp;implemented with Payment Pages 2.0. While retaining the benefits of the existing&nbsp;Payment Pages 1.0,&nbsp;Payment Pages 2.0 are&nbsp;more flexible to configure, easier to localize and customize on the fly, and faster to implement.&nbsp;The following are some of the new features related to Payment Pages 2.0:</p> <ul> <li> <p dir="ltr">Simpler configuration with&nbsp;a&nbsp;button to retrieve Payment Page&nbsp;settings from Zuora.</p> </li> <li> <p dir="ltr">More flexible configuration options,&nbsp;such as inline or overlay rendering,&nbsp;than&nbsp;the previous hosted payment pages</p> </li> </ul> <p dir="ltr">Currently, you can use&nbsp;both Hosted Payment Pages&nbsp;1.0&nbsp;and Payment Pages 2.0. However, we recommend that you upgrade and use Payment Pages 2.0 going forward.</p> <p dir="ltr">To use Payment Pages 2.0 in Zuora Quotes, your tenant must have&nbsp;Payment Pages 2.0 enabled in Zuora.</p> <p dir="ltr">See&nbsp;<a title="Payment Pages Settings" rel="internal" href="https://zuora-prod2.mindtouch.us/CB_Commerce/Zuora_for_Salesforce/C_Z-Force_Quotes/B_Zuora_Quotes_Configuration_Settings/K_Configure_Zuora_Payment_Pages_2.0_for_Zuora_Quotes">Payment Pages Settings</a>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<a title="PaymentPage Component" rel="internal" href="https://zuora-prod2.mindtouch.us/CB_Commerce/Zuora_for_Salesforce/E_Z-Force_Builder/B_Component_Library/Global_Components/PaymentPage_Component">PaymentPage Component</a>&nbsp;for detail information about setting up and using Payment Pages 2.0 in Zuora Quotes.</p> </div><div id="section_6" class="mt-page-section"><span When this setting is selected, your users will not hit the 100 Billing Accounts limit on the Select Billing Account page.</p> <p>Additionally,&nbsp;you can add custom fields to the Billing Account
